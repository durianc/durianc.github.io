## Publications

### Masked Theme-Specific Named Entity Recognition Assisted with Large Language Models
**Weiyu Chen**, et al.  
*IEEE International Conference on Computer and Communications (ICOCO), 2024*

- Proposed an **unsupervised framework for domain-specific named entity recognition** using **large language models and knowledge graphs**.
- Constructed a domain knowledge base using **LLMs and Wikipedia category structures**.
- Designed a **two-stage entity discovery pipeline** combining vector retrieval and novel entity detection.
- Achieved **80% increase in discovered entities** and **64.3% improvement in recognition precision** in specialized domain documents.

DOI: https://doi.org/10.1109/ICOCO62848.2024.10928264
