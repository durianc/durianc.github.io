I am currently an **MSc (Eng) student in Robotics and Intelligent Systems at the University of Hong Kong (HKU)**. I received my **BEng in Computer Science and Technology from Fuzhou University**, graduating as an **Outstanding Graduate**.

My research centers on **embodied intelligence**, with a particular interest in how large models can integrate **perception, reasoning, and action** to support intelligent agents in complex environments. I am especially interested in **causal world models** and **vision-language-action (VLA) systems**, as well as their role in improving adaptability, generalization, and decision-making in embodied settings.

More broadly, I also work on **visual understanding and multimodal AI**, with experience in tasks that connect language and vision for real-world applications. In parallel, I am interested in **large language models and knowledge-enhanced retrieval**, including retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and structured knowledge systems for domain-specific intelligence.

My research and engineering experience spans **natural language processing, computer vision, and human–AI interaction**. I have worked on projects involving **causal representation learning for world models, multimodal image understanding, knowledge-enhanced entity recognition, and recommendation systems**, which together shaped my broader view of intelligent system design across perception, reasoning, and application layers.

I also gained industry experience as an **AI Engineer (NLP) intern at Huawei**, where I worked on **large-model-driven fault diagnosis and case recommendation systems**. My work included building instruction-tuning datasets from large-scale ticket logs, designing root-cause extraction pipelines, and constructing interpretable fault trees for enterprise AI applications.

My long-term goal is to **bridge foundation models and embodied intelligence**, enabling autonomous agents to operate more effectively in dynamic real-world environments.

## Research Interests

- Causal world models  
- Embodied AI and intelligent agents  
- Vision-Language-Action (VLA) systems  
- Visual understanding and multimodal AI  
- Large language models and knowledge-enhanced retrieval
